Privacy Policy for Brightli

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

Last Updated: August 7, 2025

Introduction

Brightli ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our personal planning and reflection app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Our Core Privacy Promise: Brightli is designed with privacy at its foundation. All your personal data, including your activities, ratings, reflections, and insights stays on your device or in your personal cloud storage. We do not have access to, collect, or store your personal content on our servers.

Information We Do and Do Not Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect or Access

Your Personal Content: We cannot access your scheduled activities, task lists, activity ratings, reflection responses, or any insights generated by the app

Your Usage Patterns: We do not track how you use the app, which features you access, or your behavioral patterns within the app

Location Data: We do not collect or access your location information

Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, including device identifiers, operating system details, or hardware specifications

Photos or Media: We do not access your photos, camera, or other media files

Contact Information: We do not access your contacts or communication history

Information We May Collect

Account Registration: If you choose to provide your name during the app's onboarding process, this information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

App Store Information: We may receive basic information from the App Store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) related to your app download and subscription status, in accordance with standard app store practices. This may include:

Basic subscription status (active, expired, etc.)

App version information

General geographic region (country level only)

How Your Data is Stored

Local Device Storage

All your personal data is stored directly on your mobile device using secure local storage methods. This includes:

Your activities and schedules

Activity ratings and feedback

Reflection responses and insights

App preferences and settings

Any patterns or analytics generated by the app

Cloud Storage (Your Personal Cloud)

When you enable device backup features (such as iCloud for iOS devices), your Brightli data may be backed up to your personal cloud storage account. This backup is:

Controlled entirely by you and your device's backup settings

Encrypted according to your cloud provider's security standards

Not accessible to Brightli or any third parties

Managed under your cloud provider's privacy policy (not ours)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties because we do not have access to your personal data in the first place.

No Third-Party Services: Brightli does not integrate with or send data to:

Analytics services

Advertising networks

Social media platforms

Marketing services

Data brokers

Any other third-party services

No Government or Law Enforcement Access: Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, we cannot provide your personal information to government agencies or law enforcement, even if requested.

Data Security

Since your data never leaves your control, you maintain complete security over your information. We recommend:

Keeping your device updated with the latest security patches

Using device lock features (passcode, fingerprint, face recognition)

Regularly backing up your device to your personal cloud storage

Being mindful of who has access to your unlocked device

Your Data Rights and Control

Complete Ownership

You have complete ownership and control over all your data because it resides solely on your device.

Data Export

Premium Feature: Data export functionality is available to Premium subscribers. You can export your reflection responses and activity data directly from within the app. Exported data includes:

Your reflections and responses

Created activities with scheduling information

Activity ratings and feedback

All associated metadata

Free Tier Users: While export is a Premium feature, you maintain complete control over your data through the app's interface and can always upgrade to Premium for export functionality.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

Delete from App: Use the app's built-in deletion features to remove specific activities, reflections, or all data

Complete Removal: Uninstall the app from your device to remove all locally stored data

Cloud Backup Removal: Manage your cloud backups through your device's backup settings

No Account Deletion Needed

Since we do not maintain user accounts or store your data on our servers, there is no account deletion process required.

Children's Privacy

Brightli can be used by individuals of all ages. However, since we do not collect personal information from users of any age, we do not have specific procedures for handling children's data. All data remains on the user's device regardless of age.

Parents and guardians should be aware that:

Any data entered by children remains on the device

Parents control access through device parental controls

Parents manage any cloud backup of the app's data through device settings

Changes to Data Practices

If we ever change our approach to data collection or storage, we will:

Update this Privacy Policy with clear notice of changes

Provide prominent notification within the app

Give users the opportunity to review and accept any new data practices

Maintain our commitment to user privacy and control

International Data Considerations

Since your data remains on your device:

No international data transfers occur through Brightli

Your data is subject to the laws of your jurisdiction

Any cloud backup transfers are governed by your cloud provider's policies

You maintain complete control regardless of your location

Technical Implementation

App Store Compliance

Brightli complies with Apple App Store and Google Play Store privacy requirements while maintaining our commitment to local data storage.

Updates and Sync

App updates do not affect your stored data

No synchronization occurs with external servers

All data remains local to your device and personal cloud backups

Contact Information

Email: info@brightliapp.com

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), even though our local-first data approach means we collect minimal personal information.

Your CCPA Rights:

Right to Know: You can request information about what personal information we collect, use, or disclose

Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information that we may have

Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of the sale of your personal information

Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

How These Rights Apply to Brightli:

Information We Collect: We collect minimal personal information as described in this policy

No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information to third parties

Local Data Storage: Since your app data stays on your device, you maintain direct control over your information

Deletion: You can delete your data directly through the app or by uninstalling the app

Exercising Your Rights:

To exercise your CCPA rights or ask questions about our data practices, contact us at info@brightliapp.com. Please note that since your personal app data is stored locally on your device, most data management must be performed by you using the app's built-in features.

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable privacy laws including:

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Other applicable state and federal privacy laws

Our local-first approach inherently provides the highest level of privacy protection, often exceeding the requirements of these regulations.
