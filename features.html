Features

Brightli Features

Discover everything Brightli offers to support your growth journey

Planner

Your central hub for scheduling and managing all your activities.

Today's Tasks

The main view shows your activities scheduled for today, giving you a clear snapshot of what's ahead. Each activity displays its category, time, and completion status at a glance.

Multiple Layouts

  • Card Layout: Simple display for your scheduled activities
  • Timeline Layout: Activities arranged chronologically with time markers
  • Compact Layout: Condensed list view with important details

Calendar Views

Switch between weekly and monthly calendars to plan ahead or review past activities.

Reflections & Journal

The reflection system is the heart of Brightli's intelligence.

What Are Reflections?

Thoughtful prompts that appear after you mark tracked activities as complete. They help you consider how you experienced the activity and what could be better.

How It Works

Each reflection begins with a 1-5 star rating, then assigns an appropriate question based on your activity type, category, and past patterns.

Journal Entries

Beyond activity reflections, create journal entries to capture thoughts, gratitude, or daily observations that complement your growth journey.

Data Insights

Transform your reflections into actionable patterns and personalized guidance.

Understanding the Insight Tabs

  • Overview: Your headline insights and overall patterns
  • Performance: Completion rates, consistency streaks, and activity distribution
  • Mood & Energy: How activities and times affect your wellbeing
  • Patterns: Deeper behavioral trends and contextual insights

Getting the Best Insights

Complete reflections for at least 10-15 activities, be honest in your ratings, and track a variety of activity types to see cross-category patterns.

Customizations

Make Brightli feel like yours with personalization options.

Theme Selection

Choose from over 20 beautiful themes to match your mood and style. Free users have access to 3 core themes; Premium unlocks the full collection.

Activity Layouts

Switch between Card, Timeline, and Compact layouts to match how you prefer to view your activities.

Category Sets

Choose from Default (Happy, Strong, Organized, Smart), Lifestyle (Heart, Body, Life, Mind), or Creative (Connect, Move, Create, Think) frameworks.

Activity Autocomplete

Automatically mark overdue activities as complete to keep your schedule accurate without manual cleanup.

Daily Quotes

Start your day with inspiration from a curated collection of motivational quotes.

Quote System

Brightli displays a new inspiring quote on your main dashboard. Free plan shows quotes every Sunday; Premium users can customize the schedule.

iCloud Sync

Premium

Keep your data synchronized across all your Apple devices.

How It Works

When enabled, iCloud Sync automatically backs up your activities, reflections, journal entries, and settings to your iCloud account. Switch seamlessly between devices.

Privacy First

Your data syncs directly to your personal iCloud account, not to Brightli's servers. You maintain complete control and ownership.

Tip

Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all devices where you want to use Brightli.

Advanced Reflection System

Premium

Experience intelligent question selection that adapts to your patterns.

How It Adapts

The advanced system analyzes your activity history, previous ratings, and reflection responses to ask increasingly relevant questions.

300+ Premium Questions

Gain access to an expanded question database covering energy management, social dynamics, creative flow, stress responses, and long-term goal alignment.

Notifications & Reminders

Stay on track with customizable activity reminders and reflection nudges.

Activity Reminders

Set reminders for individual activities so you never miss out. Choose to be notified at a specific time before an activity starts.

Reflection Reminders

Gentle reminders to complete pending reflections for tracked activities, helping you maintain the habit that powers your insights.

Daily Quote Notifications

Optionally receive your daily quote as a notification to start your day with inspiration.
