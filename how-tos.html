Step-by-step instructions for getting the most out of Brightli

üì• How to Download Your Reflections & Journal Entries

Export your data to keep a backup or analyze your growth journey outside the app.

Step-by-Step:

  1. Tap the Settings icon in the top-right corner of the main screen
  2. Select Account from the settings menu
  3. Scroll down to the Data Management section
  4. Tap Export Data
  5. Choose which data you want to export:
    • Activities & Tasks
    • Reflections
    • Journal Entries
    • All of the above
  6. Select your preferred format (CSV, JSON, or PDF)
  7. Tap Generate Export
  8. Choose how to share or save the file (email it to yourself, save to Files app, etc.)
üìç Note:

Your exported data includes all local data and, if you have iCloud Sync enabled, your cloud-synced data as well. The export is formatted for easy readability and can be opened in spreadsheet applications or text editors.

üí≠ Understanding the Reflection Flow

Learn how reflections work and how to make them most valuable.

When Do Reflections Appear?

Reflections are triggered when you mark a tracked activity as complete. If you've enabled "Activity Tracking" when creating an activity, Brightli will prompt you to reflect after you complete it.

The Reflection Process:

  1. Mark Complete: Tap the checkmark on a tracked activity
  2. Rate Your Experience: You'll see a 1-5 star rating scale - tap to rate how the activity went
  3. Answer Questions: Based on your activity and rating, Brightli presents 2-3 thoughtful questions
  4. Respond Thoughtfully: Type your responses - these are for you, so be honest
  5. Save or Skip: Tap Save Reflection to keep it, or Skip for Now if you'll reflect later

Different Response Path Options:

Brightli adapts its questions based on your rating:

  • High Ratings (4-5 stars): Questions focus on what went well, what factors contributed to success, and how to replicate this experience
  • Mid Ratings (3 stars): Questions explore what was okay versus what could improve, helping you optimize
  • Low Ratings (1-2 stars): Questions help identify obstacles, energy drains, or circumstances that made the activity challenging

How to Approach Reflection Responses:

‚úÖ Be Specific:

Instead of "It was good," try "I felt energized because I started early and the weather was perfect." Details help you identify patterns.

‚úÖ Note Context:

Mention factors like time of day, who you were with, your mood beforehand, weather, location - anything that might have influenced your experience.

‚úÖ Be Honest:

Your reflections are private. Being truthful (even about struggles or frustrations) gives you better insights over time.

‚úÖ Keep It Reasonable:

You don't need to write paragraphs. A sentence or two with key details is often enough to capture the essence of your experience.

Skipped Reflections:

If you skip a reflection, it moves to your Pending Reflections list in the Reflections tab. You can complete it anytime - Brightli saves the activity details so you can reflect when you're ready.

‚úÖ How to Complete Reflections

Whether you're catching up on pending reflections or completing a new one.

For New Activities:

  1. Complete a tracked activity by tapping its checkmark
  2. The reflection prompt appears immediately
  3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
  4. Answer the questions that appear
  5. Tap Save Reflection

For Pending Reflections:

  1. Tap the Reflections tab at the bottom of the screen
  2. You'll see two sections: Completed and Pending
  3. Tap on any activity in the Pending Reflections list
  4. The reflection prompt opens with the activity details
  5. Rate and answer the questions
  6. Tap Save Reflection
üí° Finding Older Activities:

If you're looking for an activity from a previous day to reflect on, you have a few options:

  • Go to the Tracking tab and switch to Week or Month view to see past activities
  • Use the calendar to select a specific date and view that day's activities
  • Scroll through past weeks to locate the activity
  • Once you find it, tap the activity and mark it complete
  • Then head to Reflections ‚Üí Pending to complete the reflection

üóëÔ∏è How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. Delete specific items or everything at once.

Delete Individual Items:

To Delete a Single Activity:

  1. Find the activity you want to delete in your planner or calendar view
  2. Tap on the activity to open its details
  3. Tap the Three Dots menu icon (‚ãØ) or Edit
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Activity
  5. Confirm the deletion

To Delete a Single Reflection or Journal Entry:

  1. Go to the Reflections tab
  2. Find the reflection or journal entry you want to delete
  3. Tap on it to open the details
  4. Tap the Three Dots menu icon (‚ãØ)
  5. Select Delete Reflection or Delete Entry
  6. Confirm the deletion

Delete Some Data (Bulk Delete):

  1. Tap Settings in the top-right corner
  2. Select Account
  3. Scroll to the Data Management section
  4. Tap Manage Data
  5. Choose what you want to delete:
    • All Activities
    • All Reflections
    • All Journal Entries
    • Data from specific date range
  6. Tap Delete Selected
  7. Confirm by typing "DELETE" when prompted

Delete All Data (Complete Reset):

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning:

This action cannot be undone. Make sure to export your data first if you want to keep a backup.

  1. Tap Settings in the top-right corner
  2. Select Account
  3. Scroll to the bottom to find Advanced Options
  4. Tap Delete All Data
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. If you're sure, type "DELETE ALL" to confirm
  7. Tap Permanently Delete

What Gets Deleted:

  • Local Data: All data stored on your device is immediately deleted
  • iCloud Data: If you have iCloud Sync enabled, your cloud data is also deleted. This affects all devices synced to your iCloud account
  • Settings: Your app settings, themes, and preferences are reset to defaults (unless you choose to keep settings)
üìç Fresh Start Option:

If you want to start fresh but keep your settings and preferences, choose Delete Activities & Reflections Only instead of a complete data deletion. This preserves your app configuration while clearing your activity history.
