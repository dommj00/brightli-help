How to Download Your Reflections & Journal Entries

Export your data to keep a backup or analyze your growth journey outside the app.

Step-by-Step:

  1. Tap the Settings icon in the bottom-right corner of the main navigation bar
  2. Select Data Export from the settings menu
  3. Select the Reflections or Journal button
  4. Select one or more journal entries or reflection responses
  5. Tap Export Data
  6. Choose how to share or save the file (email it to yourself, save to Files app, etc.)

Understanding the Reflection Flow

Learn how reflections work and how to make them most valuable.

When Do Reflections Appear?

Reflections are available when you mark a tracked activity as complete. If you've enabled "Activity Tracking" when creating an activity, Brightli will prompt you to reflect after you complete it.

The Reflection Process:

  1. Mark Complete: Tap the activity and select the three dots in the activity details pop up screen
  2. Start Reflection Flow: Find the activity in the Pending Reflections section or the Pending tab in the Reflections tile and tap on it to start the reflection flow
  3. Rate Your Experience: You'll see a 1-5 star rating scale, tap to rate how the activity went (long-press for half-star ratings)
  4. Answer Questions: Based on your activity and rating Brightli will select an appropriate question
  5. Respond Thoughtfully: Type your responses, these are only for you so be honest
  6. Save or Skip: Tap Submit/strong> to save your reflection

Different Response Path Options:

Brightli adapts its questions based on your rating:

  • High Ratings (4-5 stars)
  • Mid Ratings (3 stars)
  • Low Ratings (1-2 stars)

How to Approach Reflection Responses:

Be Specific:

Instead of "It was good," try "I felt energized because I started early and the weather was perfect." This level detail is more helpful in identifying patterns.

Note Context:

Mention factors like time of day, who you were with, your mood beforehand, weather, location or anything else that might have impacted your experience.

Be Honest:

Your reflections are private. Being truthful (even about struggles or frustrations) gives you better insights over time.

Keep It Reasonable:

You don't need to write paragraphs. A sentence or two with key details is often enough to capture the essence of your experience.

Skipped Reflections:

If you assign your reflection a rating, but Skip the response, this activity will still generate insights. However, for best results, always submit a reflection response. You can choose the 'Something Different' Reflection Question option if you want to keep it short.

How to Complete Reflections

Whether you're catching up on pending reflections or completing a new one.

For New Activities:

  1. Complete a tracked activity by tapping the activity
  2. Then select Complete from the three-dot menu in the pop up screen
  3. The reflection flow begins immediately
  4. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
  5. Answer the questions that appear
  6. Tap Submit

For Pending Reflections:

  1. Tap the Reflections tile
  2. Select the Pending tab in the Reflections screen
  3. Tap on any activity in the list
  4. The reflection prompt opens with the activity details
  5. Rate and answer the questions
  6. Tap Submit

  • Scroll through past weeks to locate the activity

You have full control over your data. Delete specific items or everything at once.

Delete Individual Items:

To Delete a Single Activity:

  1. Find the activity you want to delete on your main screen or calendar
  2. Tap on the activity to open its details
  3. Tap the Three Dots menu icon (‚ãØ) or Edit
  4. Tap Delete Activity

Delete Reflections & Journal Entries:

  1. Go to the Settings icon on in the navigation bar
  2. Select Data
  3. Go to the Data Export section
  4. Tap the journal entries or reflections you wish to delete
  5. Tap Delete Selected
  6. Confirm by typing "DELETE" when prompted

Delete All Data (Complete Reset):

Warning:

This action cannot be undone. Make sure to export your data first if you want to keep a backup.

Full Data Deletion

  1. Tap Settings on the navigation bar
  2. Scroll to the bottom to find Clear All Data
  3. If you're sure, type "DELETE" to confirm
  4. Tap Delete

What Gets Deleted:

  • Local Data: All data stored on your device is immediately deleted
  • Settings: Your app settings, themes, and preferences are reset to defaults
üìç Fresh Start Option:

If you want to start fresh but keep your settings and preferences, choose Delete Activities, Insights, or Journal entries in Settings > Account instead of a complete data deletion. This preserves your app configuration while clearing your history.
