How-To Guides

Step-by-step instructions for getting the most out of Brightli

How to Download Your Reflections & Journal Entries

Export your data to keep a backup or analyze your growth journey outside the app.

Step-by-Step:

Follow these steps to export your data:

1. Open Settings

Tap the Settings icon in the bottom-right corner of the main navigation bar

2. Navigate to Data Export

Select Data Export from the settings menu

3. Choose Your Data Type

Select the Reflections or Journal button

4. Select Entries

Select one or more journal entries or reflection responses

5. Export

Tap Export Data and choose how to share or save the file (email it to yourself, save to Files app, etc.)

Note

Your exported data includes all local data that was stored on your device.

Understanding the Reflection Flow

Learn how reflections work and how to make them most valuable.

When Do Reflections Appear?

Reflections are available when you mark a tracked activity as complete. If you've enabled "Activity Tracking" when creating an activity, Brightli will prompt you to reflect after you complete it.

The Reflection Process

1. Mark Complete

Tap the activity and select the three dots in the activity details pop up screen

2. Start Reflection Flow

Find the activity in the Pending Reflections section or the Pending tab in the Reflections tile and tap on it to start the reflection flow

3. Rate Your Experience

You'll see a 1-5 star rating scale, tap to rate how the activity went (long-press for half-star ratings)

4. Answer Questions

Based on your activity and rating Brightli will select an appropriate question

5. Respond Thoughtfully

Type your responses, these are only for you so be honest

6. Save or Skip

Tap Submit to save your reflection

Different Response Paths

Brightli adapts its questions based on your rating:

High Ratings (4-5 stars): Questions focus on what made the experience successful and how to replicate it.

Mid Ratings (3 stars): Questions explore what could have been better and what worked well.

Low Ratings (1-2 stars): Questions help identify obstacles and consider alternatives.

How to Approach Reflection Responses

Be Specific

Instead of "It was good," try "I felt energized because I started early and the weather was perfect." This level of detail is more helpful in identifying patterns.

Note Context

Mention factors like time of day, who you were with, your mood beforehand, weather, location or anything else that might have impacted your experience.

Be Honest

Your reflections are private. Being truthful (even about struggles or frustrations) gives you better insights over time.

Keep It Reasonable

You don't need to write paragraphs. A sentence or two with key details is often enough to capture the essence of your experience.

Skipped Reflections

If you assign your reflection a rating but skip the response, this activity will still generate insights. However, for best results, always submit a reflection response. You can choose the 'Something Different' reflection question option if you want to keep it short.

How to Complete Reflections

Whether you're catching up on pending reflections or completing a new one.

For New Activities

1. Complete the Activity

Complete a tracked activity by tapping the activity

2. Mark as Complete

Select Complete from the three-dot menu in the pop up screen

3. Reflection Begins

The reflection flow begins immediately

4. Rate & Respond

Rate your experience (1-5 stars), answer the questions that appear, and tap Submit

For Pending Reflections

1. Open Reflections

Tap the Reflections tile

2. Go to Pending Tab

Select the Pending tab in the Reflections screen

3. Select Activity

Tap on any activity in the list

4. Complete Reflection

The reflection prompt opens with the activity details. Rate and answer the questions, then tap Submit

Finding Older Activities

If you're looking for activities from a previous day or week to reflect on, you have a few options:

  • Go to the Tracking tab and switch to the Week or Month tab to see past activities
  • Use the Calendar to select a specific date and scan the list of activities for that day
  • Scroll through past weeks to locate the activity
  • Once you find it, tap the activity and mark it complete
  • Then head to Reflections → Pending to complete the reflection

How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over your data. Delete specific items or everything at once.

Delete Individual Activities

1. Find the Activity

Find the activity you want to delete on your main screen or calendar

2. Open Activity Details

Tap on the activity to open its details

3. Delete

Tap the Three Dots menu icon (⋯) or Edit, then tap Delete Activity

Delete Reflections & Journal Entries

1. Open Settings

Go to the Settings icon in the navigation bar

2. Navigate to Data Export

Select Data, then go to the Data Export section

3. Select Items

Tap the journal entries or reflections you wish to delete

4. Delete Selected

Tap Delete Selected and confirm by typing "DELETE" when prompted

Delete All Data (Complete Reset)

Warning

This action cannot be undone. Make sure to export your data first if you want to keep a backup.

1. Open Settings

Tap Settings on the navigation bar

2. Find Clear All Data

Scroll to the bottom to find Clear All Data

3. Confirm Deletion

Type "DELETE" to confirm and tap Delete

What Gets Deleted

Local Data: All data stored on your device is immediately deleted

Settings: Your app settings, themes, and preferences are reset to defaults

Fresh Start Option

If you want to start fresh but keep your settings and preferences, choose Delete Activities, Insights, or Journal entries in Settings → Account instead of a complete data deletion. This preserves your app configuration while clearing your history.
