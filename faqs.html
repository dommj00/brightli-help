Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Brightli

What is Brightli? ▼
Brightli is an activity planning and intelligent reflection app that helps you close the productivity loop. Unlike traditional productivity apps that only help you schedule tasks, Brightli prompts you with thought-provoking reflection questions to help you understand what works for you and adapt your habits accordingly.
How is Brightli different from other productivity apps? ▼
Most productivity apps stop at planning and tracking. Brightli completes the loop by helping you reflect on your experiences, discover patterns in your behavior, and gain insights about what activities work best for you. It's not just about doing more—it's about understanding what makes you thrive.
What are reflection questions? ▼
Reflection questions are personalized prompts that appear after you complete tracked activities. They help you think about your experience, mood, energy levels, and what factors contributed to your success or challenges. These questions adapt based on your responses and activity patterns.
How do I complete reflections? ▼
After completing a tracked activity, you'll see a reflection prompt asking you to rate your experience (1-5 stars) and answer 2-3 context-aware questions. Simply rate how it went, respond thoughtfully to the questions (be honest—they're private!), and tap "Save Reflection." You can also complete pending reflections anytime by going to the Reflections tab.
I can't find activities from previous days to reflect on. How do I locate them? ▼
There are several ways to find older activities:
  • Go to the Tracking tab and switch to Week or Month view to see past activities
  • Use the calendar picker to select a specific date and view that day's activities
  • Scroll through past weeks to locate the activity you're looking for
Once you find it, tap on the activity and mark it complete. Then head to Reflections → Pending Reflections to complete your reflection for that activity.
My iCloud sync isn't working. What should I do? ▼
If iCloud sync isn't working, try these steps:
  1. Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all your devices (Settings → Your Name → iCloud)
  2. Verify that iCloud Drive is enabled for Brightli (Settings → Your Name → iCloud → Manage Storage → Brightli)
  3. Check that you have available iCloud storage space
  4. Try toggling iCloud Sync off and back on in Brightli (Settings → Sync → iCloud Sync)
  5. Ensure all devices are running the latest version of iOS and Brightli
  6. Give it a few minutes—initial sync can take time depending on your data size
If problems persist after trying these steps, contact support and we'll help troubleshoot further.
Is my data private? ▼
Yes, absolutely! Brightli is built with privacy at its core. All your data—activities, reflections, journal entries, and insights—is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync (a Premium feature), your data syncs to your personal iCloud account, not Brightli's servers. You have complete control and ownership of your information.
What's the difference between Free and Premium? ▼
Free users get: Unlimited activity scheduling, 3 tracked activities per week with basic reflections, access to 3 themes, basic insights, and local data storage.

Premium users ($4.99/month) get: Unlimited tracked activities and reflections, 300+ premium reflection questions, access to all 40+ themes, advanced insights and analytics, iCloud sync across devices, data export capabilities, and priority support.
Why aren't my insights showing up? ▼
Insights require enough data to identify meaningful patterns. You'll need to complete at least 10-15 activities with reflections before Brightli can generate reliable insights. Also, make sure you're rating your activities and answering reflection questions thoughtfully—this helps the algorithm understand your patterns better.
Can I change my activity categories? ▼
Yes! You can choose different category frameworks in Settings → Customization → Activity Categories. Brightli offers category sets optimized for different focuses (wellness, productivity, creativity). Switching between them is seamless and maintains your reflection history and insights.
How do I delete my data? ▼
You have several options:
  • Delete individual items: Tap on any activity or reflection and select "Delete"
  • Delete bulk data: Go to Settings → Account → Data Management → Manage Data
  • Delete everything: Settings → Account → Advanced Options → Delete All Data
Remember to export your data first if you want to keep a backup! For detailed instructions, visit our How-To guide.
Can I export my data? ▼
Yes! Premium users can export their activity history, reflections, and insights data in various formats (CSV, JSON, PDF) for personal record-keeping or analysis in other tools. Go to Settings → Account → Export Data to access this feature.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can reach our support team through the Help Center or by visiting our support page. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For immediate help, check out our comprehensive guides in the Help Center.
