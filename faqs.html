Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Brightli

What is Brightli? ▼
Brightli is an activity planning and intelligent reflection app that helps you close the productivity loop. Unlike traditional productivity apps that only help you schedule tasks, Brightli prompts you with thought-provoking reflection questions to help you understand what works for you and adapt your habits accordingly.
How is Brightli different from other productivity apps? ▼
Most productivity apps stop at planning and tracking. Brightli completes the loop by helping you reflect on your experiences, discover patterns in your behavior, and gain insights about what activities work best for you. It's not just about doing more—it's about understanding what makes you thrive.
What are reflection questions? ▼
Reflection questions are personalized prompts that appear after you complete tracked activities. They help you think about your experience, mood, energy levels, and what factors contributed to your success or challenges. These questions adapt based on your responses and activity patterns.
Is Brightli free to use? ▼
Brightli offers both free and premium tiers. The free version includes basic activity planning, reflection tracking, and limited insights. Premium unlocks unlimited tracked activities, advanced analytics, premium themes, cloud sync across devices, and data export features.
What platforms is Brightli available on? ▼
Brightli is currently available on iOS (iPhone and iPad). An Android version is in development and coming soon. Sign up for updates on our social media channels to be notified when Android launches.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Yes! All your personal data, including activities, reflections, and insights, stays on your device or in your personal iCloud storage. We don't have access to your personal content, and we never sell your data. Privacy is built into Brightli's foundation.
How do insights work? ▼
Brightli analyzes your reflection data to identify patterns in your behavior, mood, and activity effectiveness. After consistently tracking activities and completing reflections for about a week, you'll start seeing insights about when you perform best, which activities boost your mood, and personalized recommendations for optimizing your routine.
Can I sync my data across multiple devices? ▼
Yes! Premium users can enable iCloud sync to seamlessly access their activities, reflections, and insights across all their iOS devices. Your data syncs automatically and securely through your personal iCloud account.
What are activity categories? ▼
Brightli organizes activities into four categories: Strong (physical activities), Smart (mental wellness and learning), Happy (social connections and joy), and Organized (productivity and tasks). This helps you maintain balance across different areas of your life and see where you're investing your time.
How do I get started with Brightli? ▼
Getting started is simple! Download Brightli from the App Store, complete the quick onboarding, and schedule your first activity with tracking enabled. When you complete the activity, you'll receive your first reflection prompt. Continue tracking activities and completing reflections to start discovering patterns and gaining insights about what works best for you.
Can I export my data? ▼
Yes! Premium users can export their activity history, reflections, and insights data in various formats (CSV, PDF) for personal record-keeping or analysis in other tools.
How do I contact support? ▼
You can reach our support team through the Help Center or by visiting our support page. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For immediate help, check out our comprehensive guides in the Help Center.
