üîß Common Sync Problems & Solutions

1. iCloud Not Enabled for Brightli

Problem: Brightli doesn't have iCloud access permissions.

Solution:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Select iCloud
  4. Scroll down and find Brightli
  5. Toggle it ON
  6. Return to Brightli and check sync status

2. iCloud Storage Full

Problem: Your iCloud storage is at capacity.

Solution:

  1. Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage
  2. Check available space
  3. Delete unnecessary backups or upgrade iCloud storage
  4. Brightli uses minimal storage (typically under 10MB)

3. Poor Internet Connection

Problem: Weak or unstable internet preventing sync.

Solution:

  1. Check your WiFi or cellular connection
  2. Try switching between WiFi and cellular
  3. Move closer to your WiFi router
  4. Sync will resume automatically when connection improves

4. Different Apple IDs

Problem: Using different Apple IDs on different devices.

Solution:

  1. Ensure all devices use the same Apple ID
  2. Check Apple ID in Settings > [Your Name]
  3. Sign out and back in with the correct Apple ID if needed
  4. Wait 5-10 minutes for sync to resume

5. App Not Updated

Problem: Running different versions of Brightli on different devices.

Solution:

  1. Open the App Store on all devices
  2. Search for Brightli
  3. Update to the latest version if available
  4. Restart the app after updating

üîÑ Manual Sync Troubleshooting

1Force Refresh Sync

  1. Go to Settings > iCloud Sync in Brightli
  2. Tap "Sync Now" button
  3. Wait for the sync to complete
  4. Check if data appears on other devices

2Restart Brightli

  1. Close Brightli completely (swipe up and flick away)
  2. Wait 10 seconds
  3. Reopen the app
  4. Check sync status

3Reset iCloud Sync

  1. In Brightli Settings, tap "Reset Sync"
  2. Confirm the reset
  3. Wait 2-3 minutes for re-initialization
  4. Check sync status across devices
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Resetting sync will not delete your data, but it may take 5-10 minutes for all devices to sync completely.

‚è∞ Sync Timing & Expectations

Normal Sync Timing

  • Activity completions: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
  • Reflection responses: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
  • Settings changes: 1-3 minutes
  • Large data updates: 2-5 minutes

Factors Affecting Sync Speed

  • Internet connection quality
  • iCloud server load
  • Amount of data being synced
  • Device performance
üí° Pro Tip: Sync works best when all devices are connected to WiFi and have good internet connectivity.

üîç Advanced Troubleshooting

For Persistent Sync Issues

1Check iOS System Status

Visit apple.com/support/systemstatus to verify iCloud services are operational.

2Reset Network Settings

Go to iOS Settings > General > Transfer or Reset iPhone > Reset > Reset Network Settings. This will remove saved WiFi passwords.

3Sign Out and Back In to iCloud

  1. Go to iOS Settings > [Your Name]
  2. Scroll down and tap "Sign Out"
  3. Wait 30 seconds
  4. Sign back in with your Apple ID
  5. Re-enable Brightli in iCloud settings
