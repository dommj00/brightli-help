üí≥ Subscription Management

How to Subscribe

  1. Open Brightli and go to Settings
  2. Tap Account
  3. Select "Upgrade to Premium"
  4. Choose your subscription plan
  5. Complete purchase through the App Store
  6. Premium features activate immediately

Managing Your Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Select Subscriptions
  4. Find Brightli and tap it
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription
üí° Cancellation: You can cancel anytime through iOS Settings. Premium features remain active until the end of your current billing period.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I cancel Premium?

You'll keep Premium features until your current billing period ends. After that, you'll return to the free tier with access to core features and 3 themes.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly plans?

Yes! Change your subscription plan anytime through iOS Settings > Subscriptions. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

Do my reflection and activity data stay if I cancel?

Yes! All your activities, completions, and reflections remain in the app. You'll just lose access to advanced analytics and extended history views.

Can I use Premium on multiple devices?

Absolutely! Premium subscription covers all devices logged in with the same Apple ID. iCloud sync keeps everything in perfect harmony.

Is there a family sharing option?

Currently, Brightli subscriptions are individual. We're exploring family sharing options for future updates.
