Progress Insights Troubleshooting

Understanding why insights might not appear and how to fix it

üìä What Are Progress Insights?

Progress insights are personalized reports that analyze your activity patterns and provide recommendations based on your behavior and reflection data. They help you understand:

  • Which activities boost your mood most
  • Your most productive times of day
  • Patterns in your completion rates
  • Recommendations for improving your routine

üìã Requirements for Progress Insights

üìÖMinimum Data Period

Insights require at least 7 days of activity data to generate meaningful patterns.

‚úÖActivity Completions

You need at least 10 completed activities across the analysis period.

üí≠Reflection Data

At least 5 reflection responses are needed to analyze mood and satisfaction patterns.

üéØActivity Variety

Activities should span at least 2 different categories (Mind, Body, Heart, Home) for comparative insights.

üö´ Common Reasons Why Insights Don't Generate

1. Insufficient Activity Data

Problem: You haven't completed enough activities yet.

Solution:

  • Complete at least 1-2 activities daily for a full week
  • Focus on consistency rather than quantity
  • Mark activities as complete when you finish them

2. Limited Reflection Responses

Problem: You've been skipping reflection prompts.

Solution:

  • Answer reflection prompts when they appear
  • Even quick 5-second responses help build data
  • Enable notifications to catch reflection opportunities

3. New User Account

Problem: You've recently started using Brightli.

Solution:

  • Be patient - insights appear after 7-14 days of consistent use
  • Focus on building a routine first
  • Track various types of activities for richer data

4. Activities Not Completed

Problem: Activities are scheduled but not marked complete.

Solution:

  • Remember to tap the completion circle after finishing activities
  • Check your today view for any unmarked completions
  • Set reminders to help you remember to mark completion

5. Single Category Focus

Problem: All your activities are in one category.

Solution:

  • Try activities from different categories (Mind, Body, Heart, Home)
  • This creates comparison data for more meaningful insights
  • Balance is key for comprehensive analysis

üîß Quick Diagnostic Checklist

Run through this checklist to see if you're ready for insights:

Data Requirements Check
