üîÑ Building Habits

Repeating Activities

Turn one-time activities into habits:

  • When creating an activity, enable "Repeat"
  • Choose daily, weekly, or custom patterns
  • Activities will automatically appear on scheduled days

Tracking Streaks

Watch your consistency grow:

  • Streaks show how many days in a row you've completed activities
  • Build momentum by maintaining your streaks
  • Don't worry if you miss a day - just restart tomorrow!
