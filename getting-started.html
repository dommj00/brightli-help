Getting Started with Brightli

Your complete guide to closing the productivity loop

Welcome to Brightli!

Brightli is more than just a productivity app—it's your personal growth companion that helps you understand what truly works for you. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start your journey.

Step 1: Download and Install

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Search for "Brightli"
  3. Tap "Get" to download and install the app
  4. Open Brightli from your home screen
💡 Pro Tip: Make sure you have iOS 14.0 or later for the best experience.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

When you first open Brightli, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

  • Enter Your Name: Personalize your experience by adding your first name
  • Choose Your Goals: Select what you'd like to focus on (habits, productivity, wellness, etc.)
  • Set Preferences: Choose your notification preferences and preferred activity categories
  • Pick a Theme: Select a color theme that resonates with you

Step 3: Understanding Activity Categories

Brightli uses activity categories to help you organize the different areas of your life.

How Categories Work

When you create an activity, you assign it to a category. This does two things:

  • Organization: Categories help you see at a glance how you're spending your time across different areas
  • Reflection Intelligence: The category selected determines which reflection questions you'll receive which makes them more relevant to that type of activity

Flexibility & Benefits

One of Brightli's unique strengths is its flexible category system. You can organize your activities in different ways depending on your current focus:

  • Focus on wellness dimensions (mind, body, social connections)
  • Organize by life roles (career, personal development, family)
  • Track creative pursuits separately from routine tasks

The beauty is that switching between different category frameworks is seamless and designed to continue your progress. Your reflection history and insights adapt intelligently so you never lose the patterns you've built.

Example in Action:

Let's say you schedule "Morning Workout" and assign it to a physical wellness category. After completing it, Brightli might ask: "How did your energy level feel during this workout?" or "What factors made this session easier or harder than usual?"

These questions help you discover patterns like realizing you always feel more energized when you exercise before 8am, or that outdoor workouts boost your mood more than gym sessions.

Category-Driven Insights

As you complete activities and reflect on them, Brightli analyzes patterns within and across categories. You might discover:

  • Which types of activities consistently boost your mood
  • What time of day you're most productive for different activity types
  • How balancing different life areas affects your overall wellbeing
💡 Getting Started Tip:

Don't overthink category selection when you're beginning. Pick what feels right intuitively. You can always adjust later and Brightli's system is designed to maintain continuity between category set switches.

Step 4: Schedule Your First Activity

Ready to close your first loop? Here's how to create a tracked activity:

  1. Tap the + button at the bottom of your screen
  2. Enter a title for your activity (e.g., "Morning Run," "Team Meeting")
  3. Select a category that best fits this activity
  4. Set the date and time
  5. Add the expected duration
  6. Toggle "Activity Tracking" ON to enable reflection questions
  7. Optionally add notes
  8. Tap "Save"
Important: Make sure to enable "Activity Tracking" if you want to receive reflection prompts and gain insights from this activity!

Step 5: Complete and Reflect

This is where the magic happens—closing the productivity loop.

Mark Activity Complete

  1. When you finish your activity, tap on the activity card in your main screen view
  2. Tap the three dots in the pop up and select Complete
  3. It will mark as complete and move it to the Completed tile
  4. If tracking is enabled, you can complete a reflection by tapping the Reflect button in the Pending Reflections section

The Reflection Flow

  1. Rate Your Experience: Rate your activity 1-5 stars
  2. Select a Response Type:You may choose to write a freeform response, receive a reflection prompt, or skip a reflection this time.
  3. Be Honest: Your reflections are private. Authenticity leads to better insights
  4. Save: Submit your reflection to complete the productivity loop
Why Reflect?

Reflections transform activities from checkboxes into learning opportunities. Over time, your reflections reveal patterns you'd never notice otherwise. For example, you may discover that you're more creative after morning walks, or that certain environments consistently drain your energy.

Step 6: Review Your Insights

After completing several activities with reflections, head to the Insights tab to see what Brightli has learned about you.

  • View completion patterns and consistency trends
  • Discover mood and energy patterns across activities
  • Identify optimal times for different types of tasks
  • See which activities contribute most to your wellbeing
Pro Tip: Insights become more accurate and meaningful with more data. Aim for at least 10-15 reflected activities to start seeing valuable patterns.

Tips for Success

🌱 Start Small

Begin with 2-3 activities per day with at least 3 tracked activities per week. As you build the habit, you can expand to more activities.

Be Specific

Instead of "Exercise," try "30-min Morning Run" or "Yoga Session." Specific titles help you track patterns better.

Use Notes

Add context in the notes field: weather, who you were with, your starting mood. This helps you identify factors that impact your experience.

Set Reminders

Enable notifications so you don't forget to complete activities and reflections.

Review Weekly

Spend 5 minutes each week reviewing your insights and going over submitted reflection responses. Look for patterns and opportunities to optimize.
