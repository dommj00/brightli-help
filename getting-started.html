Getting Started with Brightli

Your complete guide to closing the productivity loop

Welcome to Brightli!

Brightli is more than just a productivity app‚Äîit's your personal growth companion that helps you understand what truly works for you. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start your journey.

Step 1: Download and Install

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Search for "Brightli"
  3. Tap "Get" to download and install the app
  4. Open Brightli from your home screen
üí° Pro Tip: Make sure you have iOS 14.0 or later for the best experience.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

When you first open Brightli, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

  • Enter Your Name: Personalize your experience by adding your first name
  • Choose Your Goals: Select what you'd like to focus on (habits, productivity, wellness, etc.)
  • Set Preferences: Choose your notification preferences and preferred activity categories
  • Pick a Theme: Select a color theme that resonates with you

Step 3: Understand Activity Categories

Brightli organizes your activities into four main categories to help you maintain balance:

üí™ Strong

Physical activities like exercise, sports, and movement. Track your workouts, runs, yoga sessions, and any physical wellness activities.

üß† Smart

Mental wellness and learning activities. This includes reading, meditation, studying, courses, and cognitive development.

üòä Happy

Social connections and joyful experiences. Time with friends, hobbies, creative pursuits, and activities that bring you joy.

‚úÖ Organized

Productivity and task management. Work projects, household chores, errands, and organizational tasks.

Step 4: Schedule Your First Activity

Ready to close your first loop? Here's how to create a tracked activity:

  1. Tap the + button at the bottom of your screen
  2. Enter a title for your activity (e.g., "Morning Run," "Team Meeting")
  3. Select a category (Strong, Smart, Happy, or Organized)
  4. Set the date and time
  5. Add the expected duration
  6. Toggle "Activity Tracking" ON to enable reflection questions
  7. Optionally add notes
  8. Tap "Save"
üéØ Important: Make sure to enable "Activity Tracking" if you want to receive reflection prompts and gain insights from this activity!

Step 5: Complete Your Activity

When it's time for your activity:

  1. You'll receive a notification reminder (if enabled)
  2. Open Brightli and find your activity on the dashboard
  3. Tap the three dots menu on the activity
  4. Select "Complete" when you're done

Step 6: Answer Reflection Questions

This is where the magic happens! After completing a tracked activity, you'll receive personalized reflection questions:

What to Expect:

  • Rate Your Experience: How did you feel during and after the activity?
  • Energy Levels: Did this activity boost or drain your energy?
  • Mood Impact: How did this affect your overall mood?
  • Context Questions: What factors contributed to your experience?

How to Complete Reflections:

  1. Go to the Reflections tab in your navigation
  2. You'll see pending reflections for completed activities
  3. Tap "Reflect" on a pending reflection
  4. Answer the questions honestly‚Äîthere are no wrong answers!
  5. Add any freeform notes if you'd like
  6. Tap "Complete Reflection"
üí≠ Reflection Tip: Be honest with yourself. The more authentic your responses, the more accurate and helpful your insights will be. Don't overthink it‚Äîyour first instinct is usually right!

Step 7: Discover Your Insights

After consistently tracking activities and completing reflections for about a week, you'll start seeing personalized insights:

Types of Insights:

  • Best Times: When you perform best for different types of activities
  • Mood Boosters: Which activities consistently improve your mood
  • Energy Patterns: What activities energize or drain you
  • Success Factors: Environmental and contextual factors that lead to better outcomes
  • Recommendations: Personalized suggestions based on your patterns

Accessing Insights:

  1. Tap the Insights card on your dashboard
  2. View weekly, monthly, or custom time range insights
  3. Explore different categories to see category-specific patterns
  4. Use these insights to adapt and optimize your routine

Step 8: Build Your Habit

To get the most out of Brightli, consistency is key:

  • Track Regularly: Try to track at least one activity per day
  • Complete Reflections: Don't skip reflection prompts‚Äîthey're the key to insights
  • Review Insights: Check your insights weekly to identify trends
  • Adapt: Use what you learn to make informed changes to your routine
  • Experiment: Try activities at different times or in different contexts

Tips for Success

‚ú® Start Small

Begin by tracking just 1-2 activities per day. As you build the habit, you can expand to more activities.

üéØ Be Specific

Instead of "Exercise," try "30-min Morning Run" or "Yoga Session." Specific titles help you track patterns better.

üìù Use Notes

Add context in the notes field: weather, who you were with, your starting mood. This helps identify factors that impact your experience.

‚è∞ Set Reminders

Enable notifications so you don't forget to complete activities and reflections.

üîÑ Review Weekly

Spend 5 minutes each week reviewing your insights. Look for patterns and opportunities to optimize.
