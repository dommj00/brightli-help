‚ú® How to Complete Reflections

1Read the Prompt Carefully

Take a moment to understand what the reflection is asking. The questions are designed to help you think about your experience.

2Be Honest

Your reflections are private and for your benefit. Honest responses lead to better insights about your patterns and progress.

3Don't Overthink

Your first instinct is usually the most accurate. Spend 10-30 seconds reflecting, then provide your response.

4Submit Your Response

Tap your rating, select your option, or type your thoughts, then submit. The data is saved immediately.

üí° Pro Tip: Consistent reflection over time creates the most valuable insights. Even quick 5-second responses are helpful!

‚ùì Common Questions

Why don't I see reflections after every activity?

Brightli uses smart algorithms to show reflections at meaningful moments. Too many reflections can become tedious, so we balance frequency with value.

Can I go back and add reflections I missed?

Currently, reflections are tied to the moment of activity completion for authenticity. Focus on future reflections rather than past ones.

Are my reflections private?

Yes! Your reflection data is completely private and used only to generate your personal insights. We never share or analyze individual responses.

What if I don't want to answer a reflection?

Reflections are always optional. You can dismiss them without penalty, though consistent responses provide better insights.
