Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Our Core Privacy Promise: Brightli is designed with privacy at its foundation. All your personal data, including your activities, ratings, reflections, and insights stays on your device or in your personal cloud storage. We do not have access to, collect, or store your personal content on our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Personal Content: Activities, reflections, or insights
  • Usage Patterns: How you use the app or which features you access
  • Location Data
  • Device Information
  • Photos or Media
  • Contact Information

How Your Data is Stored

Local Device Storage: All your personal data is stored directly on your mobile device using secure local storage methods.

Cloud Storage (Your Personal Cloud): When you enable device backup features (such as iCloud for iOS devices), your Brightli data may be backed up to your personal cloud storage account. This backup is encrypted and only accessible by you through your personal account credentials.
