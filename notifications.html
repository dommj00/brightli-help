‚öôÔ∏è Setting Up Activity Reminders

1Enable During Activity Creation

When scheduling a new activity:

  1. Tap the + button to create an activity
  2. Fill in the activity details
  3. Toggle "Reminder" to ON
  4. Choose how many minutes before: 5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes
  5. Save the activity

2Add Reminders to Existing Activities

For activities already created:

  1. Tap on the activity to view details
  2. Tap "Edit"
  3. Toggle "Reminder" to ON
  4. Select your preferred timing
  5. Save changes
üí° Smart Timing: 15-minute reminders work well for most activities. Use 30-60 minutes for activities requiring preparation.

üì± iOS Notification Permissions

1Initial Permission Setup

When you first open Brightli, you'll be asked for notification permission. Always choose "Allow" to receive reminders.

2If You Denied Permission

To enable notifications later:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Brightli
  3. Tap Notifications
  4. Toggle "Allow Notifications" to ON
  5. Choose your preferred alert style

3Customize iOS Notification Style

In iPhone Settings > Brightli > Notifications:

  • Lock Screen: Show notifications when locked
  • Notification Center: Keep notifications in center
  • Banners: Choose temporary or persistent alerts
  • Sounds: Enable notification sounds
  • Badges: Show red number on app icon

üîá Managing Notification Frequency

If You're Getting Too Many Notifications

Premium users can:

  • Reduce reflection reminder frequency
  • Set longer quiet hours
  • Disable certain notification types
  • Customize timing for each category

All users can:

  • Turn off individual activity reminders
  • Schedule fewer activities with reminders
  • Use iOS Do Not Disturb for quiet times
  • Disable notifications entirely in iOS Settings

If You're Not Getting Enough Notifications

  • Add reminders to more activities
  • Check iOS notification permissions
  • Ensure Brightli is allowed during Do Not Disturb
  • Verify notification timing settings

üö´ Troubleshooting Notification Issues

Notifications Not Appearing

Check these settings:

  1. iOS Settings > Brightli > Notifications - Ensure enabled
  2. Do Not Disturb - Check if DND is blocking notifications
  3. Low Power Mode - Can delay notifications
  4. App permissions - Verify Brightli has notification access
  5. Time zone - Ensure device time zone is correct

Wrong Notification Timing

Possible causes:

  • Device time zone incorrect
  • Activity scheduled in wrong time zone
  • iOS notification delay due to system load
  • Notification timing setting misconfigured

Notifications Appearing Late

Solutions:

  • Restart your iPhone
  • Close and reopen Brightli
  • Check for iOS updates
  • Ensure good internet connectivity
